Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading hub for fitness, recreation, and community engagement, offering world-class facilities and programs to empower individuals to lead active and healthy lifestyles.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide state-of-the-art fitness and sports facilities, innovative programs, and a supportive community environment that encourages growth, fun, and well-being for all members.

Our Founder

AI Sports Complex was founded by Aman Izzat, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for sports and fitness. Aman has dedicated his life to promoting health and fitness through innovative recreational spaces and programs. His leadership and commitment have driven AI Sports Complex to become the hub of fitness, community, and sportsmanship in Kulim.

With years of experience and expertise in fitness and community-building, Aman Izzat ensures that every facility, program, and initiative is aimed at creating the ultimate sports and fitness experience for every member and visitor.
